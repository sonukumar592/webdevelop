Article: The Role of Artificial Intelligence in Education

Article:

Artificial intelligence(AI) is transforming the education sector worldwide. from personalised learning platforms to smart assessment systems, AI is helping teachers and students achieve better outcomes.personalised learning adapts study materials according to each students's pace and understanding. AI-powered tools, such as chatbots and virtual assistants, support student by answering queries instantly. in addition, predictive analytics can identify struggling students early, allowing timely interventions. However, challenges such as data privacy, teacher training and excessive dependence on technology need to be addressed. if used responsibly, AI can make education more inclusive, efficient, and engaging.
